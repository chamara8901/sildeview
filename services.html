Services

Nichols Quality Painting is a locally owned and operated company providing services to Northern Colorado and surrounding areas (from Lafayette, CO to Cheyenne, WY). We use only top-of-the-line products to ensure quality, longevity, and that only the highest standards are met. We ensure professionalism, cleanliness, complete customer satisfaction, friendliness, quality work, and respect for you and your home. Quality preparation is essential. NQP provides a thorough and detailed preparation process of all projects.

  • Exterior Painting
  • Stain
  • Refinishing
  • Decks

  • Pressure Washing
  • Epoxy Garage Floors
  • Epoxy Countertops
  • Custom Driftwood Art
