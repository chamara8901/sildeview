Services

Nichols Quality Painting is a locally owned and operated company providing services to Northern Colorado and surrounding areas (from Lafayette, CO to Cheyenne, WY). Whether it's residential or commercial, interior or exterior, painting or epoxy resurfacing, Nichols will bring our experience, professionalism and quality work to your next project.

We use only top-of-the-line products to ensure quality, longevity, and that only the highest standards are met. We ensure cleanliness, complete customer satisfaction, friendliness, quality work, and respect for you and your home. Quality preparation is essential, so NQP provides a thorough and detailed preparation process of all projects.

Nichols Quality Painting owner and operator Jerry Nichols will handle your work personally. Since he doesn't have to pay a fleet of painters, that overhead savings is passed onto you. This means your money goes further with Nichols.

If you have a question about the process or the work being done, Jerry will be there to walk you through the details and next steps. That's the personal touch you don't get with the bigger paint companies.
